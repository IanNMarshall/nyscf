About:

This website can be used for searching and visualizing New York State campaign finance data from 1999-Present. All data is sourced from New York States election site. I do not personally guarantee the accuracy of this data, please see https://www.elections.ny.gov/CFViewReports.html for details on filing and data accuracy. This site has no affiliation with New York State.

Instructions:

  • View and search a list of canditates and committees by navigating to the All Filers List in the sidebar menu or clicking here
  • Click on a Filer ID to view list of all contributions and transactions reported by that filer
  • Filter contributions to dynamically update charts.
